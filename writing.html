Cohousing Community

I was so excited to get the opportunity to work with the Independent Florida Alligator again. For this story about the first official cohousing community in the state of Florida, I got the opportunity to both report and shoot photos.

Ketamine to treat depression

This was another awesome experience to work with the Independent Florida Alligator. This story is about the use of a street drug to treat depression. Through a ton of investigative reporting, I learned that ketamine is actually more effective in treating short-term mental problems, such as suicidal thoughts.
