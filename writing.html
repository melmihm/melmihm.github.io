BudgetVegHead

I started this blog for a class and have had so much fun doing it. It's a mix of cheap and easy vegan recipes, as well as reviews on vegan restaurants. In addition, I give tips on the best vegan grocery items and how to make the most out of dried herbs and spices. My goal is to help people realize that vegan food isn't bland or expensive. Rather, it can be incredibly cheap and delicious.
